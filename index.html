Hello Bicycle-Campers

We Want You

Swift Industries invites you to pack up and skip town for a solstice bike-overnight.

Swift Campout is pedaling to camp with all of your belongings strapped to your bicycle, and setting up camp in a serene spot with the sound of frogs chirping in the distance. Swift Campout is an ode to the simple pleasure of camp coffee, fly fishing at dawn, it’s rediscovering the lost art of lying in a field braiding tall-grass. Swift Campout is a celebration of the longest day and the first summer night.

Wherever you may be, ride your bike to camp and settle in for a night under starry skies. Go explore the coast range of California, hang a hammock in the Umatilla wilderness, or wake to camp-coffee at Stargazer Farm Camp. Discover the solitude of an uncharted spot or head into the hills with a bunch of friends. Your call.

This is about autonomy and sense-of-place.
